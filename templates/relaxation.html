Mind Relaxation Games

Breathing Exercise

Follow the circle: Breathe in as it expands, out as it contracts.

Color Memory

Remember the sequence of colors and click them in order.

Positive Affirmations

Click the button to receive a positive affirmation.

Sudoku

Fill in the grid so that every row, column, and 3x3 box contains the digits 1-9.

Crossword

Solve the crossword puzzle by filling in the words based on the clues.

N-Queens (8-Queens Puzzle)

Place 8 queens on the board so that no two queens threaten each other.

Sliding Puzzle

Arrange the tiles in numerical order by sliding them into the empty space.
